IBTrACS data source note
﻿
The real-world experiment in this study uses the International Best Track Archive for Climate Stewardship (IBTrACS) tropical cyclone dataset.
﻿
Dataset version:
IBTrACS v04r01
﻿
Local source file used in this study:
ibtracs.last3years.list.v04r01.csv
﻿
Official data source:
NOAA National Centers for Environmental Information (NCEI)
International Best Track Archive for Climate Stewardship (IBTrACS)
https://www.ncei.noaa.gov/products/international-best-track-archive
﻿
Use in this study:
The IBTrACS data were used to evaluate clustering behavior under periodic longitude boundary conditions. A subset of tropical cyclone trajectories crossing or approaching the longitude seam was extracted for the real-world validation experiment.
